# Hub71 Abu Dhabi — Pitch Research for Ideation Stage
> Team: 3 founders | IIT Madras | Chemical Engineering + ML background

---

## What is Hub71?

Hub71 is Abu Dhabi's global tech ecosystem, backed by **Mubadala Investment Company**, **Microsoft**, and **SoftBank**. It operates out of **Abu Dhabi Global Market (ADGM)** on Al Maryah Island — one of the most business-friendly financial free zones in the world.

- 400+ active startups from 50+ countries
- Portfolio companies have collectively raised **$4 billion+** in funding
- Acceptance rate as low as **1.1%** (Cohort 18) — very competitive

---

## Program Best Suited for Your Stage: **Initiate**

**Initiate** is Hub71's program specifically built for **ideation and pre-MVP founders** — exactly where your team is.

### What it offers
- Connection with experienced **venture builders** (SC Ventures + VentureOne)
- Help to **validate your idea**, structure the business model, build MVP
- Office space at Hub71, Abu Dhabi
- Access to Hub71's **150+ corporate and investor partners**
- Clear pathway into the **Access Programme** for continued growth

### Who runs it
- **SC Ventures** — innovation arm of Standard Chartered Bank
- **VentureOne** — global venture builder
- Also: **Fikra Ventures** partnered with Hub71 to build AI-native companies from Abu Dhabi

---

## Next Stage After Initiate: **Access Programme**

Once you graduate from Initiate, you can move to the Access Programme which gives:

| Benefit | Amount |
|---|---|
| In-kind incentives (service credits) | AED 250,000 (~$68,000) |
| Cash via SAFE note | AED 250,000 (~$68,000) |
| **Total package** | **AED 500,000 (~$136,000)** |
| Top-up (for high performers) | Up to AED 250,000 more |

- SAFE note is **founder-friendly**, structured under ADGM law
- 3-month guided track **powered by Techstars**
- Equity is only taken when you raise a formal funding round

## How to Position Your Team

### Strengths to highlight
| Your Profile | How Hub71 Sees It |
|---|---|
| IIT Madras | World-class technical institution, signals research depth |
| Chemical Engineering | Deep tech credibility — materials, energy, bioprocesses |
| ML expertise | Aligns with Hub71's AI-first priorities |
| 3 co-founders | Hub71 values team completeness at ideation stage |
| India base | Active India-UAE corridor, existing precedent |

### Framing suggestions
- **"Deep tech at the intersection of ChemE and AI"** — this is rare and fundable
- Connect your idea to **Abu Dhabi's Vision 2030**: energy transition, food/water security, advanced manufacturing, healthcare
- If relevant: frame around **decarbonization or net-zero** (aligns with UAE COP28 legacy and ClimateTech program)
- Emphasize that at least **one founder is willing to relocate to Abu Dhabi** (this is a firm requirement)

## Application Requirements

1. **Online application form** covering:
   - Problem you're solving
   - Your solution and product vision
   - Business model
   - How your startup contributes to Abu Dhabi's economic diversification
2. **Pitch deck** (PDF) — upload at end of form
3. At least one founder must commit to **long-term relocation to Abu Dhabi**
4. Evaluation criteria: **Team + Market Opportunity + Growth Plans**

> If you miss a cohort deadline, you're automatically considered for the next cohort — applications are rolling.

## Pitch Deck Checklist (for Ideation Stage)

- [ ] Problem — clear pain point with data
- [ ] Solution — your unique approach (lean into ChemE+ML angle)
- [ ] Why now — market timing or regulatory tailwind
- [ ] Team — IIT Madras, disciplines, relevant research/projects
- [ ] Market size — TAM/SAM/SOM
- [ ] Go-to-market — even a rough hypothesis
- [ ] What you need — what Initiate/Hub71 gives you specifically
- [ ] Abu Dhabi relevance — how this fits UAE's national priorities
